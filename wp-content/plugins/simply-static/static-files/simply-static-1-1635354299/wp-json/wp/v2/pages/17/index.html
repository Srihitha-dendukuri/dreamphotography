I HAVE TWO TITLES I HOLD DEAR MAMA & PHOTOGRAPHER.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

I am also a dreamer, a foodie, and a nerd at heart. I love laughing. I love art. I love coffee. I am a bit of a movie buff and love all things Disney and SciFi. I can’t get enough of the rain or the ocean (which might have something to do with being born and raised in Phoenix, AZ). I love the city and enjoy all that downtown Phoenix has to offer.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

I SPECIALIZE IN PORTRAIT AND LIFESTYLE PHOTOGRAPHY.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

My approach is simple and non-obtrusive. I love photographing people and want to tell your story with pure and honest images. I feel so lucky to be doing what I love.
